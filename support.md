# Support
**TVBuddy: Smart TV Companion**

If you need help using TVBuddy or have questions about the app, permissions, or compatibility, you can contact us using the details below.

## App Information
TVBuddy is a Smart TV companion app designed to connect locally to supported Smart TVs on the same Wi-Fi network. Feature availability may vary depending on TV model, operating system, and network configuration.

## Common Topics
- Connecting to a Smart TV on the same Wi-Fi network  
- Local Network and Nearby Devices permissions  
- TV pairing using on-screen codes  
- Compatibility with different TV models  

## Privacy
TVBuddy is designed to operate primarily on your local network.  
For details on how data and permissions are handled, please review our Privacy Policy:
