# Privacy Policy
**TVBuddy: Smart TV Companion**  
Last updated: January 2026

## Overview
TVBuddy: Smart TV Companion (“TVBuddy”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) is designed to operate primarily on your local network. We respect your privacy and are committed to transparency about how the app works and how data is used.

TVBuddy does not require user account creation and does not sell personal data.

## Information We Do Not Collect
TVBuddy does not collect:
- Names, email addresses, or phone numbers
- Account credentials
- Precise GPS location
- Advertising identifiers
- Browsing or viewing history

## Analytics
TVBuddy may use limited analytics to understand app performance, diagnose issues, and improve functionality.

Analytics data:
- Is used only for app performance and improvement
- Is not used for advertising
- Is not sold
- Is not shared with third parties for marketing purposes

Where possible, analytics data is collected in a privacy-respecting and aggregated manner.

## Limited Location Information
TVBuddy may access **approximate location information** as required by the operating system to support local network discovery.

This information:
- Is used only to determine network availability and device connectivity
- Is not used to track users
- Is not stored
- Is not shared with third parties

TVBuddy does not access precise location data.

## Local Network & Nearby Devices
TVBuddy requests access to:
- Local Network
- Nearby Devices

These permissions are required to discover compatible Smart TVs and establish direct local connections. All communication occurs locally within your network.

## TV Pairing
Some TVs may require a temporary on-screen pairing code. This code is used only to confirm the local connection and is not stored or transmitted externally.

## Data Storage
TVBuddy does not store personal data on external servers. Any temporary data required for functionality remains on the device and is cleared when no longer needed.

## Children’s Privacy
TVBuddy does not target children and does not knowingly collect information from anyone under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected on this page with a revised date.
