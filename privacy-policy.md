# Privacy Policy
**TVBuddy: Smart TV Companion**  
Last updated: January 2026

## Overview
TVBuddy: Smart TV Companion (“TVBuddy”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) is designed to operate locally on your network. We respect your privacy and are committed to being transparent about how the app works and what data is used.

TVBuddy does not create user accounts and does not collect or sell personal information.

## Information We Do Not Collect
TVBuddy does **not** collect:
- Names, email addresses, or phone numbers
- User accounts or login credentials
- Precise GPS location
- Advertising identifiers
- Browsing or viewing history
- Personal usage analytics

## Limited Location Information
TVBuddy may access **approximate location information** as required by the operating system to support **local network discovery**.

This information is:
- Used only to determine network availability and device connectivity
- Required by the system when discovering devices on the same Wi-Fi network
- Not used to track your location
- Not stored
- Not shared with third parties
- Not used for advertising or analytics

TVBuddy does **not** access precise location data.

## Local Network & Nearby Devices Access
TVBuddy requests access to:
- **Local Network**
- **Nearby Devices**

These permissions are required to:
- Discover compatible Smart TVs on the same Wi-Fi network
- Establish a direct local connection between your phone and the TV

All communication occurs **locally** within your network. No device discovery data is transmitted to external servers.

## TV Pairing
Some TVs may display a temporary pairing code to confirm the connection. This code is used only for local authentication and is not stored or transmitted externally.

## Third-Party Libraries
TVBuddy uses system and open-source libraries solely to enable:
- Network discovery
- Connectivity checks
- Secure local communication

These libraries do not collect personal data and are not used for tracking purposes.

## Data Storage
TVBuddy does not store personal data on external servers. Any temporary data used for app functionality remains on the device and is cleared when no longer needed.

## Children’s Privacy
TVBuddy does not target children and does not knowingly collect information from anyone under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
